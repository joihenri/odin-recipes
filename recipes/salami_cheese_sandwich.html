Salami and Cheese Sandwich

Description

Everyone has their simple af sandwich that they grew up eating throughout their childhood and you'll still eat it as an adult. We had our signature sandwich: some deli meat, some sliced cheese, some spread, nothing too fancy. For me, it was a salami and cheese sandwich. Again, not fancy at all.

Very simple, takes like 5 seconds to do, but why not just share my favorite childhood sandwich? Let's bring on the nostalgia!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread, toasted
  • Mayo
  • Extra Sharp Cheddar Cheese, sliced
  • Salami straight from the deli

Steps

  1. Spread your mayo on both slices of bread, one side each.
  2. Assemble your cheese and salami together in between the bread.
  3. Eat and feel like you're enjoying your lunch on a Saturday afternoon in 1993.
